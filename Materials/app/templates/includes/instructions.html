Memory task instructions

Welcome and thanks for agreeing to take part. The study will take place in a few distinct parts:

  • First, you'll see a list of words shown on screen. Try to remember them.
  • Second, you'll watch a short video to have a break.
  • We'll show you another list of words, and you'll decide whether you saw them in the first list (or not). You'll also tell us how sure you are about each decision.
  • Then, you'll finish the task.

You'll have more detailed instructions before each step, and we'll explain the decisions in more detail after you've had the video break.We'll start by showing you a demo of how you will see the words you will be asked to remember.
